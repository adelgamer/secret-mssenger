Frequently Asked Questions

Q: "How do I send anonymous messages?"

A: To send anonymous messages, the recipient must have a Mourassil account and share their profile link with you. Once they've done so, you can send them anonymous messages.

Q: "Can others see my received anonymous messages?"

A: No, messages sent to you remain confidential. Only you have access to and can view your received messages.

Q: "Can I reply to my anonymous messages?"

A: While you can't directly reply to anonymous messages, you can share them on your social media story along with your response.

Q: "Can I send images through anonymous messages?"

A: For security reasons, only text messages are supported to prevent the sharing of inappropriate content.

Q: "Is there a mobile app for Mourassil?"

A: Currently, we do not offer a mobile app. You can access Mourassil through your web browser.

Q: "What does Mourassil mean?"

A: "Mourassil" is derived from the Arabic word "مراسل," which translates to "correspondent" in English.
