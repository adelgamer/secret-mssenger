How does Mourassil work?

Mourassil is so simple and easy to use :

  1. First create an account.
  2. Copy your profile link and post it on your social media’s (Facebook, Instagram, Snapshot…) stories/posts/Bio and write a good caption to call people to send you messages.
  3. When someone sends you a secret message you will receive an email notifying you about your received secret message.
  4. Then just log into your account and see your received anonymous message!
  5. Maybe you would want to reply to the secret message you received in a social media story!
  6. And congratulations ! Now people can be more honest with you !

Frequently asked questions

Q: "How do I send anonymous messages?"

A: You can't send anonymous messages unless that person you want to send the message to has an account in Mourassil and shared the link of his profile with you.

Q: "Can other people see the anonymous messages that were sent to me when they are going to send me a message?"

A: No, the people that are going to send you an anonymous message via the link you shared with them can't see your received messages, No one can see your messages, you only can see them.

Q: "Can i reply to my anonymous messages?"

A: No you can't reply to your secret message unless you take a screenshot of the message and post it with your reply to your social media story.

Q: "Can I send anonymous pictures or images?"

A: No, you can't because some people may send inappropriate pictures.

Q: “Is there an app for this website?”

A: No, we don't have an app.

Q: "What does Mourassil mean?"

A: "Mourassil is an Arabic word "مراسل" which means correspondent."
